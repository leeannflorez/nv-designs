Kelepona

The Case

Kelepona came to us as a brand new company. They wanted to create an awareness for their new line of phone cases, specializing in customizable cases. The owner, Mary, specifically stated that she wanted a design that was "Brightly colored and fun".

The Solution

We took that idea and transformed their web presence into a bright, Hawaiian fun. (Kelepona means phone in Hawaiian).

The Outcome

We used the same color theme as well as the same type faces across all social media creations, the website and the mobile app. We also creatd a new colorful logo for their brand to reflect not only the theme of the brand but also to modernize their look.

Chordis

The Case

Chordis Custom Guitars was in business and their pre-made sales were flourishing. However, their custom shop just wasn't making the sales that it used to when they first opened. The owners, a husband and wife team, wanted to give their web presence an overhaul to create more of a focus on their custom shop.

The Goals

Create a new mobile and large screen website that would entice users to go to their custom shop. A new layout for the custom shop page was also needed. Lauren, one of the owners, wanted to create a more vintage vibe for their company image.

The Outcome

We created a new website that had large buttons and methaphorical arrows to point customers to the custom shop. The website also held a vintage feel using photos of vintage wood (some of the wood was the actual wood used to create the guitars). We also created a cohesive user experience with the look, feel and usability of the website on both mobile and large screen.
