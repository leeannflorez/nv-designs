About Us

NV: We're not in Nevada

In 2016, when Alex Garner graduated with his Master's from Johsnon & Wales University, he knew right away that he wanted to start a new design firm with only JWu graduates. NV Designs was formed in a small office rented in East Greenwich.

The business has grown quickly in two years and has now moved to downtown Providence to better serve the city and state of Rhode Island as well as many other places.

NV's ultimate goal and mission is to help local businesses grow in both their online presence and their branding capabilities. Their reach to other businesses outside of Rhode Island has just begun but there is no end in sight for this design firm.

We ♥ Our Community

We ♥ Our Community

We at NV are so passionate about helping local businesses grow that we hold yearly seminars and send out monthly newsletters to local businesses. We share as much industry tricks and tips that we can to help you even after we are done creating for you.

We want to foster as strong of a community bond as we can so if you are local, have an event you'd like for us to sponsor or participate in, message us! We'd love to work with you!
